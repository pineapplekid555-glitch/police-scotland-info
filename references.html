Key Definitions & Terminology

Custody

The detention of a person by police, typically at a police station, following arrest

Caution

Warning given to suspect informing them of their rights before questioning

Chain of Custody

Documentation of evidence handling from collection through presentation in court

Reasonable Suspicion

Standard needed for police to stop and search or arrest a person

Due Process

Fair treatment through the judicial system as guaranteed by law

Proportionality

Police response must be appropriate to the level of threat or incident
